Note:
**WHERE vs HAVING
->WHERE is used to filter rows before grouping.
->HAVING is used to filter results after GROUP BY.

**Use HAVING with aggregate functions like:
COUNT()
SUM()
AVG()
MAX()
MIN()


link: https://leetcode.com/problems/duplicate-emails/?envType=problem-list-v2&envId=database



solution:

select email from person
group by email
having count(email)>1
