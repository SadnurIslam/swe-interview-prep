Note:
-> left join: return all records from the left table and the matched records from the right table. If there is no match, the result is NULL on the right side.





Solution:

select name, bonus from Employee e 
left join Bonus b
on e.empId=b.empId
where b.bonus<1000 or b.bonus is null
